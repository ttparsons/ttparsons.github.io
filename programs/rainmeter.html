What is Rainmeter?

Rainmeter is a free windows program that lets you add customized widgets to the desktop. You can even create your own using a simple scripting language.

 

You can install it by going here.

 

Check out these websites (Rainmeterhub, customize.org) for a lot of things people have created, or check out my weather widget (left sidebar).

 

Below you can see my current rainmeter setup on my righthand monitor. It shows the date, time, weather, and has shortcuts to many of my most used apps. The circle shows the progress and album art of whatever song is currently playing in Spotify (the background wallpaper is Monaco).
